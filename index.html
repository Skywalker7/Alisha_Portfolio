Tapestry

Tapestry was a mobile and web application for storytelling. What made Tapestry unique was that it relied on a user's tap to move through a story. Because Tapestry was such a unique application, it was featured in the Apple App Store. I crafted stories for both platforms, assisted with community management, user interviews, and QA.

  • Client: Betaworks
  • Date: 2014
  • Service: Content Development, Community Management, User Experience, & Quality Assurance

Do Tank

Do Tank is a user-centered design program that brings together those who are passionate about social good. Our team worked on an OpenIdeo challenge that asked "How can we reduce waste to create a more sustainable food system?" Through user research, user interviews and prototyping, we came up with the Zero Food Waste Popup. The popup was a week long experience that guided New Yorkers on how they can take steps to reduce waste. It brought over 100 guests.

  • Client: miLES Do Tank
  • Date: 2015
  • Service: UX, Event Planning, Social Media Management & Content Development

Gf Mostly Vegetarian

GF Mostly Vegetarian is a site dedicated to mostly vegetarian cuisine that was gluten free. This project allowed me to connect with amazing people such as Shauna James Ahern and Silvana Nardone. I worked with these women and countless others on the Gluten Free Ratio Rally. The Gluten Free Ratio Rally focused on making delicious goods that didn't contain binders that are traditionally found in gluten free food.

  • Client: Alisha Austin
  • Date: 2011-2014
  • Service: Web & Content Development
